Welcome

Fang helps you stay up to date with your favorites news websites and blogs.

RSS and Atom news feeds are supported. For the latest information please visit www.GetFang.com.

Tip: To return to this screen later, press the ? button at the top of the sidebar.

Add a feed

Press the + button at the bottom of the sidebar to add a feed.

Enter the URL and press "Continue."

Once the feed is located, edit the title if you like and press "Add Feed."

Tip: If Fang wasn't able to locate an RSS or Atom news feed, don't give up! Try searching for the site's news feed exact URL on the web.

Import from other news readers

Do you already have a news reader and want to try Fang? No problem.

Most news readers (including this one) have an option to export their feed list to a file format called OPML. Check the news reader you're currently using for the option to export an OPML file.

Once you have an OPML file, back in Fang press the gear button at the top of the sidebar, then press the "Import" button under "OPML feed list."

Reading a feed

Reading the news is as easy as scrolling. Select a feed in the sidebar and scroll down as you read. A bookmark appears if you scroll back up. That's it!

Lost your place? Double-click the feed in the sidebar to jump back to the bookmark.

Tip: Some news feeds only include a preview of the article. To see the complete article, click the headline and it will open in your default web browser.

Keyboard shortcuts

↑ or ↓ Scroll up or down
→ or ← Scroll to the next or previous news item
PgUp or PgDn Scroll up or down by one screen
Home or End Scroll to the first or the bottom of the last currently loaded news item
CommandCtrlR or F5
Refresh current feed
CommandCtrlQ
Quit
